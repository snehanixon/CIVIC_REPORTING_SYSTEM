About CivicHub

CivicHub connects residents and local authorities to quickly report, track and resolve civic issues in your neighborhood. Share photos, add locations, and follow progress until it's fixed.

Submit your first report

Community Rules

We encourage respectful, accurate reporting. Avoid sharing sensitive personal data and check for duplicates before reporting.

Community Guidelines

  • Be accurate: Provide clear descriptions and a correct location—screenshots, photos and a short note help authorities act faster.
  • Be respectful: Use civil language. Harassment, hate speech or personal attacks are not permitted.
  • No spam or duplicates: Check existing reports before filing a new one. Duplicate reports may be merged.
  • Privacy first: Do not post others' personal or sensitive information without consent.
  • Follow the law: Don’t use this platform to promote illegal activity or to publish copyrighted content you don’t own.

How to report effectively

  1. Choose the right category (roads, sanitation, lighting, water, etc.).
  2. Add a concise description: what, where, and when.
  3. Attach one or more clear photos. A close-up and a wide shot together are ideal.
  4. Provide a location text or landmark. If available, include a street address or GPS coordinates.
  5. Monitor your report: officials may comment or mark it resolved; you can add updates if the situation changes.

Privacy & Safety

We take privacy seriously. Personal information in reports is visible to other users and admins—avoid sharing sensitive data. We store minimal information required to operate the service (your name, email, and reports). If you want an account deleted, contact us and we will remove your data in accordance with our policy.

Response and Follow-up

Reports are routed to local authorities where possible; response times vary by department and location. When a report is resolved, it will be marked and you may receive a notification (if you opted in).

Contact

For assistance, feedback, or to request data removal, email us at contact@civichub.example or use the contact form on this site.
